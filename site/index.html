[01] // THE PROBLEM

TINY NOTIFICATIONS VANISH BEFORE YOU LOOK UP.

You're heads-down in deep work. You context-switch all day. Time blindness is real — and a banner that slides away in four seconds was never going to save you. Klaxon takes over the entire screen, on every display, so the alert is the one thing you cannot scroll past.

[02] // WHAT IT DOES

BUILT LIKE A TOOL.

01

FULL-SCREEN ALERTS

Every connected display goes full-screen at your lead time — at start, or 1 / 2 / 5 / 10 / 15 minutes before.

02

ONE-CLICK JOIN

Detects meeting links for 30+ services — Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex, Whereby, Jitsi — from URL, location, or notes.

03

KEYBOARD-FIRST

Return joins. ⌘1/2/3 snooze. Esc dismisses. Every button shows its shortcut — hands never leave the keys.

04

CONFIGURABLE SNOOZE

Zero to three snooze buttons of your choosing (default 1 / 3 / 5 min), plus a plain Dismiss. Your call.

05

THEMES & SOUNDS

Ten gradient themes, a Random option, or your own image. Pair it with any macOS system sound — or silence.

06

CALENDAR-AWARE

Works with every account already in macOS Calendar — iCloud, Google, Microsoft/Exchange. Pick which calendars count.

[04] // VS IN YOUR FACE

A FREE, OPEN-SOURCE ALTERNATIVE TO IN YOUR FACE.

In Your Face is a good paid app — polished, cross-platform, and commercially supported. Klaxon is a free open-source alternative to In Your Face: the same core idea, a full-screen alert you cannot miss before every meeting, built for the Mac. The differences are in the model, not the mission:
